About Me

I am Dr. Tawhid Hasan, a dedicated oral and dental surgeon with comprehensive training from prestigious institutions worldwide.

As a dedicated Oral and Dental Surgeon, my practice is built on the intersection of advanced surgical technology and meticulous clinical care. I am committed to restoring both function and aesthetics for my patients.

I believe that every surgical outcome should be as predictable as it is transformative, utilizing the latest in 3D imaging and minimally invasive techniques to ensure the highest standards of safety and recovery.

My practice is built on a foundation of trust, empathy, and excellence. I believe in providing personalized care that addresses each patient's unique needs and concerns.

My approach combines advanced dental techniques with a gentle touch, ensuring that every patient feels comfortable and confident in their treatment plan.

I am committed to continuous education and staying current with the latest advancements in dentistry to provide the best possible care for my patients.
